**Meeting Overview**
The meeting focused on integrating three distinct AI agents into the company's workflows, troubleshooting current AI tests, and defining staff roles for the AI task allocation based on the company's Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs). The company currently has a staff strength of three in Singapore.

**1. AI Agent Workflows**
The team discussed the implementation and scope of three different AI agents:
*   **Deadline Reminder Agent:** This agent uses a Google Sheet to track deadlines and send reminders. Currently, the system checks for deadlines at 9:00 AM daily. Jasper noted that email reminders for the 20th and 24th of April were not received. The developer identified that the email body was empty, which prevented the system from triggering the reminder, and will investigate further.
*   **WhatsApp FAQ/Inquiry Agent:** A new phone line was acquired specifically for this AI bot. It is designed to handle direct inquiries from prospects and the website, processing both voice and text messages in English and Chinese. Initially, Jasper wanted an AI inside existing client group chats to draft replies, but it was decided to keep the FAQ inquiry agent separate because its capabilities are already extensive. The AI will learn from manual replies to build its knowledge base.
*   **Project Manager (PA) Agent:** Jasper wants this agent to act as his personal assistant to read messages and allocate tasks to specific staff members based on the workflow. Task reminders and follow-ups will be sent to staff on a daily basis.

For the Project Manager AI to distribute tasks correctly, the team clarified specific roles:
